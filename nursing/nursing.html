About Us

The Dr. Kamakshi Institute of Medical sciences & Research (DR.KIMSR) was established in the year 2010 by Dr.T.G.Govindarajan with a vision of generating required human resources to the healthcare industry. It is a unique academic institution which is committed to academic excellence. The institute is an additional facility of Dr. Kamakshi Memorial Hospital Pvt. Ltd

The DR.KIMSR is affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Dr. M. G. R Medical University under the allied health sciences stream. The various courses conducted are B.Sc & M.Sc Radiography and Imaging Technology (RIT), B.Sc Radiotherapy Technology (RTT), B.Sc Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT), B.Sc Nursing and many more such courses are in the pipeline. Similarly, DNB courses are being conducted in various specialities. The departments are headed by highly experienced teachers with rich clinical experience as well as in technology. The RIT and RTT courses are duly approved by Atomic Energy Regulatory Board, Govt. of India, so that our students are able to work throughout India. As on 2024, many students got graduation in various specialities like Radiography & Imaging Technology, Radiotherapy technology and Medical laboratory Technology from our institution. All the students have got good placement in many prestigeous institution India and abroad.

In Medical Physics itself, about 48, 35, 8 students of various Universities have completed their implant training, project dissertation and one-year internship, respectively.

In addition to excellent teaching staff in all subjects. We are also equipped with adequate and modern teaching facilities for the students.

Dr.Kamakshi College Of Nursing

Dr.Kamakshi College of Nursing is incepted under Dr.Kamakshi Healthcare and Educational Trust in the year 2024 with the admission capacity of 60 students. The institute obtained GO (Ms)No:50 from Tamilnadu State Government and affiliated under The Tamilnadu Dr MGR Medical University and recognized by Tamilnadu Nurses and Midwives Council. The institute has all the facilities like spacious classrooms, Library with recent updated books and journals, fully equipped laboratories, big multi-purpose hall, expert teaching faculties, Good Hostel Facilities, etc., to impart good education to students.

  • Admission Criteria

    • The minimum age for admission shall be 17 years, on 31st December of the year at which admission is sought. The maximum age limit for admission should be 35 years.

    • Minimum Educational Qualification

      • Candidate with Science who have passed the qualifying 12th Standard examination (10+2) and must have obtained a minimum of 45% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Biology taken together and passed in English individually. In respect of candidates belonging to SC/ST/OBC the marks obtained in 3 core subjects shall be 40% instead of 45% for General category candidates.

      • Candidates are also eligible from State Open School recognized by State Government and National Institute of Open School (NIOS) recognized by Central Government having Science subjects and English only.

      • English is a compulsory subject in 10+2 for being eligible for admission to B.Sc. (Nursing).

    • Colour blind candidates are eligible provided that colour corrective contact lens and spectacles are worn by such candidates.

    • Candidate shall be medically fit.

    • Married candidates are also eligible for admission.

    • Students shall be admitted once a year.

    • Selection of students should be based on entrance examination.

  • Examination Criteria

    • A candidate must have minimum of 80% attendance (irrespective of the kind of absence) in theory and practical in each course/subject for appearing for examination.

    • A candidate must have 100% attendance in each of the practical areas before award of degree.

    • The candidate shall appear for exams in each semester.

    • A candidate has to pass in theory and practical exam separately in each of the paper.

    • If a candidate fails in either theory or practical, he/she has to re-appear for both the papers (Theory and Practical).

    • The candidate has to pass separately in internal and external examination.

    • The candidate shall have cleared all the previous examination before appearing for final year examination.
